Kannur lies on 25m above sea level. The climate is tropical in Kannur. Rainfall is significant most months of the year, and the short dry season has little effect. This location is classified as Am by Köppen and Geiger. The average annual temperature in Kannur is 27.2 °C. In a year, the average rainfall is 3351 mm. Kannur is located towards north of Kozhikode (Calicut, India) south of Mangalore and towards the west of Western Ghats Coorg and mananthavady taluk of Wayanad.
